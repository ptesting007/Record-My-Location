Can I take notes?

While you are recording positions you can press the big area in the Capture tab to annotate something: a special location, an instance in time you want to remember for some reason, etc. A note taking screen will show up with the option to take a picture.

Since this software only exports text logs, your picture will be saved to the device's photo library, separate from the GPS locations. Still, a note will be present in the logs reminding you when/where you took it, and you can use the picture's date to match it with the note later at your computer.
